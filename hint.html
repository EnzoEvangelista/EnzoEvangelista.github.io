Hints

Instructions

1. Click the "Start" button to begin a level.

2. Follow the numbers as they count down from 1 to 5.

3. Click the boxes in order. If you click the wrong box, your progress will be restarted to the beginning.

4. If you click the boxes in the right order, you will be congratulated and moved on to the next level.

Cheatsheet

Level 1: Red, Yellow, Green, Orange, Blue
Level 2: Blue, Orange, Green, Red, Yellow, Red, Blue, Green
Level 3: Yellow, Orange, Blue, Green, Yellow, Red, Blue, Red, Green, Blue
